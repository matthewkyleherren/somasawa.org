Sunny and Ashleigh are influential business thinkers and globally recognized business advisors on vision, leadership, culture, and brand thinking. Invite one or both of them to speak, virtually or live, at your next special event and learn how to unleash big ideas, inspire innovation, and build a Culture of Possibility™.

Speaking topics
and key themes

(01)

Visionary Leadership

Vision is the highest form of leadership, yet we live in a time of leadership blindness. We speak about the limiting beliefs that prevent visionary thinking so we can cast bold vision others can buy into.

(02)

Culture of Possibility™

Leaders want people to push the envelope yet create a culture that prohibits brave thinking. We’ll teach you how to create a Culture of Possibility™ and how one visionary’s dream becomes a company’s shared creation.

(03)

Rare Breed® Innovation

There’s an overwhelming lack of acceptance for crazy ideas that don’t fit the mold. Based on Sunny and Ashleigh’s book principles, we’ll share how to leverage unconventional leadership traits to innovate like a Rare Breed.
