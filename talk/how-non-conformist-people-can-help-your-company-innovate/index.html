About the Talk

The most innovative workers approach problems and solutions with blinding originality despite all their quirks and sharp elbows. This talk shares why these “Rare Breeds” should be welcomed into teams and organizations and how best to tap their ingenuity to achieve greater innovation.

How to attract the most innovative talent.

Employees know leadership is broken, and they’re ready for a new mindset at the top.

Have you ever been told to turn it down a notch? To back down or chill out? To be less loud, less daring, less weird? Have you ever been worried you’re too much to handle or that you come across as a tad full on? Have you ever been called intense, obsessed, or rebellious?

In this inspiring keynote, Sunny Bonnell will share how Rare Breeds are the future and how organizations would do well to structure their leadership, culture, and brand on a new foundation.

Rare Breed innovators are looking for:

– An innovation culture
– A purpose
– A culture where their trait can thrive
– Encouragement to show up as they are
– Seeing possibility, not boundaries

Sunny Bonnell will explore why these “Rare Breeds” should be welcomed into teams and organizations and how best to tap their ingenuity to achieve greater innovation.
